Revenue missed expectations, as well. Avon Products &mdash; The cosmetics maker fell 8 cents a share short of estimates, with adjusted quarterly profit of 1 cent per share. Revenue also missed estimates, as sales were lower in most of its global markets. <strong>GNC Holdings</strong> &mdash; The nutritional supplements retailer earned an adjusted 7 cents per share for its latest quarter, well short of the 36 cent a share consensus estimate. Revenue also topped Street forecasts. The maker of semiconductor manufacturing equipment's revenue was in line with forecasts.
