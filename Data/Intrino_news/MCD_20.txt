Many fast food chains offer a kids meal with a kids toy. What about an adult meal with a toy? Sounds too risque and taboo? Not for Restaurant Brands International Inc (NYSE: QSR )'s Burger King franchise - in Israel of all places. Israel, home to some of the oldest and most religious sites for the major monotheistic faiths, will be offering a an adult-themed meal package. "Kids meal? That's for kids," a video of a Hebrew advertisement on the chain's Facebook page said. "Burger King presents 'The Adults Meal' with an adults toy inside. Only on Valentine's Day." The meal comes in a not so discreet dark blue box and includes a feather duster, a sleep mask and a head massages. As for the actual meal portion, there are two Whoppers, two french fries and two beers There is no word if this promotion will be brought over to the United States, although it's safe bet it never will.
