<strong>Arconic Inc</strong>. ( ARNC ) announced that it has monetized more than 60 percent of the 36.31 million shares it retained in <strong>Alcoa Corporation</strong>. The sale of 23.35 million <strong>Alcoa</strong> shares will result in about $890 million in proceeds. <strong>Arconic</strong> noted that the proceeds bolster the Company's cash balance, which provides financial flexibility to pay down debt and/or pursue share repurchases, based on a relative-return assessment. At the separation of <strong>Alcoa Inc.</strong>, <strong>Arconic</strong> chose to retain a 19.9 percent stake in <strong>Alcoa Corporation</strong>. The Company indicated it would review options for responsibly managing the stake, taking into account its continued upside potential.
