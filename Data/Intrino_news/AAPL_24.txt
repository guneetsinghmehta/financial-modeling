Taiwan, Apple s (AAPL) manufacturing backyard, is attracting a lot of interest from overseas investors lately, as the smartphone maker s stock price rushes to record highs on enthusiasm for the release of iPhone X later this year. Foreigners net bought $1.1 billion of Taiwanese stocks between February 9 and 15, a seven-month high . Year-to-date, foreigners have bought $2.5 billion of Taiwanese stocks, the most among Asian markets. Taiwan s stock market has a strong foreign presence, with a third of the trading conducted by overseas investors. This year, Largan Precision (3008.Taiwan), which makes dual cameras for Apple, has already advanced 25%. Catcher Technology (2474.Taiwan), which makes casings, has gained 22%.  The iShares MSCI Taiwan Capped ETF (EWT) is up 11.6%. Error message And the Taiwanese Dollars went up in value. But if the foreign investments suddenly pull out, it will be a disaster for the small retail investors. Be alert.
