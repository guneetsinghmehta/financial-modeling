FARMINGTON, Conn., Feb. 15, 2017 /PRNewswire/ -- A webcast of United Technologies Corp. ( UTX ) Chairman CEO Greg Hayes speaking at the Barclays Industrial Select Conference is scheduled for 11:30 a.m. ET on Wednesday, Feb. 22, 2017. The presentation will be broadcast live on the Internet at www.utc.com and will be archived on the website afterward. United Technologies Corp., based in Farmington, Connecticut, provides high-technology systems and services to the building and aerospace industries. By combining a passion for science with precision engineering, the company is creating smart, sustainable solutions the world needs.
